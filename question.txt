int a = 10, b, c;
c = ++a;
b = a++ + c--;
System.out.println(a++, "," + b++ + "," + ++c);

Step-by-step execution (VERY IMPORTANT 👇)

Statement	       a	b	c
a = 10	           10	–	–
c = ++a	           11	–	11
b = a++ + c--	   a=12	22	10

Statements               ///     Conclusion
print a++(post increment)///	prints 12 → a becomes 13		
print b++(post increment)///	prints 22 → b becomes 23		
print ++c(pre increment) ///	c becomes 11 → prints 11
